# Free vs Pro — FormulaGuard Plans

FormulaGuard helps cosmetic formulators build safe, compliant formulas with ingredient limits, EU Annex guidance, and IFRA notes. This document explains what you can do with each plan.

---

## Free Plan — What You Can Do

- **Build formulas** with the ingredient calculator
- **Check compliance** with EU Annex II (banned ingredients) and EU Annex III (restricted ingredients)
- **IFRA validation** for fragrance ingredient limits
- **Print/PDF export** your formulas
- **Calculate percentages** and batch scaling
- **Access all builder features** — add ingredients, set phases, define process steps

You can use FormulaGuard without creating an account, though signing up enables additional features.

---

## Pro Plan — What You Unlock

- **Save formulas** to your account. Your formulas are stored securely and accessible from any device.
- **Save As** to create variations of existing formulas without losing the original.
- **Dashboard** to view and manage all your saved formulas in one place.
- **Reload saved formulas** from your dashboard to continue working where you left off.

Pro is useful if you work with multiple formulas, need to iterate on recipes, or want to keep a library of your formulations.
